# English for Career Preparation

Materials and notebooks for developing professional English communication skills.

## Topics Covered

### 01 - Resume Writing
- Resume structure and formatting
- Action verbs and achievements
- ATS-friendly resumes
- Personal branding

### 02 - Cover Letters
- Cover letter structure
- Tailoring to job descriptions
- Storytelling techniques
- Common mistakes to avoid

### 03 - Interview Preparation
- Common interview questions
- STAR method for behavioral questions
- Technical interview communication
- Follow-up etiquette

### 04 - Business Communication
- Email writing best practices
- Professional messaging
- Report writing
- Meeting communications

### 05 - Presentations
- Presentation structure
- Visual aids and slides
- Public speaking tips
- Q&A handling

### 06 - Networking
- LinkedIn optimization
- Networking event strategies
- Informational interviews
- Building professional relationships

## Directories

- `exercises/` - Writing exercises and practice
- `resources/` - Templates, examples, and references

## Getting Started

1. Work through notebooks sequentially
2. Complete exercises in each section
3. Use templates in `resources/` for practice
